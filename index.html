Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has substantial potential in innovation, driven by initiatives like "Digital Bangladesh," a growing startup ecosystem, advancements in agricultural technology, and investments in renewable energy.

Sustainability

Bangladesh advances sustainability with investments in renewable energy, promoting sustainable agricultural practices, enhancing climate resilience, and improving waste management.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh invests in infrastructure, with key projects like the Padma Bridge, enhancing connectivity and trade, and the Dhaka Metro Rail, reducing traffic congestion.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh is advancing with initiatives focused on women's participation in education, the workforce, and leadership roles, fostering significant progress.

Digital transformation integrates digital technology into all business areas, changing operations and customer value delivery. It involves cloud computing, AI, IoT, and requires rethinking business models and processes.
